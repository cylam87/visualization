ACTIVITY 1: accessible visualizations (06_accessible_data_visualization.pdf, slide 11) 

In what ways is the visualization inaccessible, and to whom? 
- no values in axes - no idea about # of cases on y axis or timeline of x axis 
- red text is readable for red-green color blindness, but won’t be highlighted
the ‘uncontrolled transmission’ curve is shaded but it’s not clear why the color is darker near the top 
- text can’t be read by computer’s text reader, so no alt text can be generated for those who need it (and there’s no figure legend/caption)
- text is somewhat blurry = hard to read
- lots of different fonts (typeface, bold, colors, font size, caps) is confusing for viewers (increases cognitive load)
