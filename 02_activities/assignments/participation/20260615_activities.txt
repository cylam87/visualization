ACTIVITY 2: types of change on dynamic visualizations (09_beyond_matplotlib.pdf, slide 32) 

Types of changes that occur when you interact with this dynamic visualization? 
- transitional or immediate - when you input a different term, the order of the Y axis shifts and the scale of the X axis changes, so could be immediate
- but X and Y stay the same (uses vs discipline), so could argue for transitional

What spatial and retinal elements stay constant and what elements change? 
- retinal elements (colors, size, brightness) stay same
- spatial elements (order, x axis scale) changes
